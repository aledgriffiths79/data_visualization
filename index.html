Developed and Developing Countries Life Expectancy Correlation

This dashboard uses World Health Organisation data from 2015. The data is used to explore correlations between developed and devoloping countries and whether their gross national income per capita corrolates with life expectancy.

There is a strong correlation between gross nation income (GNI) per capita based on purchasing power parity (PPP) and life expectancy.
